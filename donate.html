Credit Card Gifts

Use the button above to connect to our secure credit card donation page, or call 860-242-9999, x304.

Recurring Gifts
You can also use the "Donate Now" button above to make a recurring gift to Our Companions. Recurring gifts allow you to make a larger commitment and space out the payments while providing continuous support to Our Companions. Check the box on the donation form that says “I would like my donation to recur.”

Memorial Donations
To have your memorial listed on our website please make your donation using our Memorial Donation page.

Gifts of Stock and Securities
Gifts of stock may be made directly to Our Companions. Please call Susan Linker at 860-242-9999 x301 for transfer instructions or contact us here.

United Way
You can direct donations to Our Companions Domestic Animal Sanctuary through your local United Way Campaign! Since Our Companions is a qualified nonprofit organization recognized as a 510(c)(3) by the Internal Revenue Service, you can write in our name and Tax ID number (41-2047734) to have your giving designated directly to Our Companions.

For more information, please call Susan Linker at 860-242-9999, x301, or contact us here.

Leadership Giving
Henry and K.K. McLane Society

Established in 2007 in memory of Our Companions' first donors, the Henry and Katharine K. McLane Society honors a distinguished group of individuals, businesses and organizations who generously support our programs and services in a leadership capacity.


Henry and Katharine K. (K.K.) McLane Society Giving Levels:

Founders’ Circle: $10,000+
Guardians’ Circle: $5,000 - $9,999
Caregivers’ Circle: $2,500 - $4,999
Companions’ Row: $1,000 - $2,499
Rescuers’ Row: $250 - $999
